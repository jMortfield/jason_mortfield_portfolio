Front-end developer

I am a front-end web developer based out of Toronto, Ontario. I have always enjoyed working in a collaborative environment, and using logic and reason to solve challenging problems. I enjoy building clean, responsive, accessible websites using the best and most up-to-date practices.

Prior to making the change into the tech industry, I graduated from the University of Guelph with a science degree, followed by the completion of a teaching degree at the University of Western Ontario. My scientific background has equipped me with the skills necessary to approach difficult challenges, such as those experienced when coding, from a rational and logical standpoint.
